Delete Account – Merit HR App

App Name: Merit HR
Developer: Merit Advisory

Merit HR is an employee management application used by organizations to manage HR-related data.

How to request account deletion

To delete your account and associated data:

Open the Merit HR app
Go to Dashboard
Tap Manage Account
Select Delete Account
Enter your password
Confirm deletion
Types of data deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently deleted:

Authentication data (managed via Firebase)
User profile information (name, email, login credentials)
App usage data linked to your account
Data that may be retained

Some data may be retained in our HR system (Odoo) for the following reasons:

Legal or organizational compliance
Employment and payroll records

This retained data will be:

Anonymized
Not linked to your personal identity
Data retention period
Retained data may be stored for up to 90 days or longer if required by organizational or legal obligations
After this period, data is securely deleted or fully anonymized
Alternative deletion request

If you are unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by contacting:

devops@meritadvisory.so

All requests are processed within 3 business days.
